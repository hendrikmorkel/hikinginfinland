From Sea to Summit - go somewhere where you haven’t been before this weekend!

News

Losing a local legend on the summit of Mt. Baldy.

Spring is not only the time where you can go backpacking in warmer temperatures again, but it’s also prime ski-touring season across the globe. Hence this issue of The Week In Review looks equally at winter and summer activities! Enjoy - and if you like it - buy me a coffee!

Does the Freeride World Tour Still Matter? It’s a valid question as riders die and weather makes some events difficult.

Austerity and Enclosure - Walking, climbing and the politics of the public is an article our friends from the UK should read, and everyone who likes to go backpacking there.

What does the ski of the future look like? DPS and Black Crows tell where they think ski design is headed.

Like your camera? It might be gone soon: The endgame for cameras is having no camera at all.

ViewRanger is becoming a social network.

Connect Bikes and Trains - Increase the Number of Cyclists and Train Passengers. Yay for more bikes!

Outside proclaims that The Future of Adventure is Female. Do you agree?

The Hiking-Obsessed Nature Lover Behind Tycho.

Like Scandinavian gear? Then you’re in for a treat: Klättermusen now sends to Australia, Canada, Korea, Hong Kong and the USA!

Only the lightest tent will do for you? You’re in luck - the 535 g light Nordisk Lofoten is in Stock at the trekking-lite-store.com.

And the Bergfreunde have the newest La Sportiva Gear in stock - get a new pair of trailrunning or climbing shoes or the discounted Spectre 2.0, my current ski-touring boot!

REI - yes, that’s the big US Outdoor Shop! - launched a new line of Co-op Cycles, which look like affordable and well-specced bikes.

Of course if you already have enough bikes, tents, shoes and outdoor clothes you just might want to learn about the Art of Enough.

Worth checking out every month: Martin’s Weitwander Rückblick. [German]

And because soon it will be winter again it’s best to start these summer trips now. Like the Swift Campout on the 24th and 25th of June!
